Overview: Inspire Ethiopia, Enactus Leicester's first international project, was instigated by two fervent pan-African economics students who are passionate about the potential of Africa as a developing continent. The project is about empowering the next generation of Ethiopia, a country that is rich in natural resources and strong in future prospects. We aim to inspire a nation, using entrepreneurial skills to encourage social enterprise as a way of aiding development in the Ethiopian community.

What has been achieved: We have solidified and sustained the link between the students in Gondar and the students in Leicester. We have created an innovative solution to real problems faced by the inhabitants of communities in Ethiopia. Through the solution, 'Meals on Wheels', we have successfully delivered our knowledge and experience about business and ideas. We also helped Gondar students to establish their very first Students' Union to create their own Enactus Team and Projects.

And now, we are looking for students who are striving to make a difference! We hope you will be the next driving force to empower!
