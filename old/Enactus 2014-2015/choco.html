Welcome, this is ChocoNet - the gateway for your creative ideas to be put into ACTION, see the IMPACT you can have on someone's life!

Interested in either: helping those in need, expanding your employability skills (regardless of your course!), business or entrepreneurship?
With ChocoNet, you can do just that, and help the local community through a creative means - you get to see the impact of your ideas in action!


Our aim:

To work with a local socially disadvantaged group of people and to EMPOWER them by providing business and entrepreneurship skills and opportunities.

What did we do last year?

We:
- worked with local Leicester Asylum Seekers.
- created and provided business workshops, until together, we created a chocolate business.
- got actively involved in the marketing, branding, finance and selling of our product.

Where are we now?

Using the aim of empowerment and the foundation of business workshops and an experienced team, we wish to:
- work with and empower new local Leicester group.
- provide a mentoring scheme and business workshops with an application of a new, creative business idea.
- continue empowering.

This is the PERFECT stage for you to have a maximum personal impact in someone's life! NOW you can help choose the local Leicester group to empower, be a mentor, get involved in rebranding provide and realise a business idea!

What skills can you take from this?

An example of a few skills we have expanded, and wish to share with you:
Media, network, formal communication, teamwork, teaching, and business - to name a few!


ChocoNet: This is the opportunity for change, the platform to put your ideas into action, and your chance to expand your employability skills - take it.
