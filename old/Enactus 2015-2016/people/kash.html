M E E T - K A S H - C H U N D O O

Position:

President

Why did you join Enactus?

I originally joined Enactus in my first year as a way to meet new people and develop on a personal level. However, I stayed for so many more reasons! It has been the single most enjoyable experience of my whole time whilst at University. I have been able to meet so many interesting individuals whilst also having a genuine positive impact on disadvantaged communities. Furthermore, I have gained some fantastic transferrable skills along the way! I would strongly recommend joining Enactus for anyone who wants to make the most out of their time at University!

What does your role involve?

My role is to facilitate the effective running of the society. I run and chair all committee meetings, and hold other committee members to account. I also oversee Project management within the team to ensure all Projects are delivering meaningful impact. It is a challenge but I am looking forward to a great year ahead.
