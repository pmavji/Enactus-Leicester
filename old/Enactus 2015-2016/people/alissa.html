Why did you join Enactus?

When I first heard about Enactus, my first thought was 'how is it possible for students like us to make a difference in issues of such magnitude?' While wanting to make a difference in people's lives is a great motivator, the idea of someone like me, tackling tribulations ranging from unemployment to poverty was daunting and at times seemed impossible. But once I started talking to the members of the society, I was sold. The enthusiasm and belief they share in helping people was infectious yet authentic. My only regret now is why didn't I meet these people in my first year!
