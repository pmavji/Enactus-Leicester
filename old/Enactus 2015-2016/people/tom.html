M E E T - T O M - P E R K S

Position:

Treasurer and Farmers' Market Project Leader

Why did you join Enactus?

Joining enactus seemed like a fun thing to do. At first, the society offered brilliant opportunities for skill development towards employment as well as the chance to meet all sorts of people from different courses and backgrounds. The projects that enactus Leicester have established in recent years, and plan to implement in the coming academic year, interested me as they offer entrepreneurial and financially sustainable answers for social issues.

What does your role involve?

My role in enactus is to help direct our funds and grants to be at their most efficient. In order for our projects to remain successful and financially stable, careful consideration is needed in all aspects of Enactus Leicester finance. Another of my personal roles in the team is the Enactus Leicester Farmers' Market. With this, I lead a small team of students to host our commercial project, generating important profit to reinvest into our other projects, emphasising the sustainability our projects encompass.
