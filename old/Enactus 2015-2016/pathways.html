P A T H W A Y S

We have just launched a brand new project called Pathways aimed at helping disadvantaged college and sixth form students in the Leicester vicinity in their transition to University and employment through workshops, competitions and mentoring.

We aspire to make a genuine impact on students' lives through imparting our own knowledge and empowering the students to explore their future prospects and helping them achieve their aspirations.

Through this project, we are exposing students to opportunities that they would not usually be exposed to by building a platform on which they can learn, develop and administer skills which Universities' and employers look for.

We have many roles available for those who want to get involved as well as having the benefit of making a real impact in the development and progression of this project.
